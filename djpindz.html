About DJ PINDZ

DJ Pindz the biggest upcoming DJ in the south coast and here is everything you need to know about him. Started to learn to DJ in his bedroom from the age of 14 and since then has developed a passion for urban music and acquired a skill in professional DJing. He’s located in the south coast of the UK but has aspirations to move up to London to spread his skills to the big urban nightclubs. DJ Pindz has managed to get opportunities in clubs such as Oceana and Switch to showcase his work before moving onto the big stage at Wireless 2019. DJ Pindz recently stated in an online interview that ‘he wants to tour Europe in 2020’. Dreaming big is his motto and he’s on the right track at the moment. You can listen to DJ Pindz mixtapes in the contact section where he demonstrates the music he performs ranging from UK Rap such as Jhus, Dave and Stormy to American Rap such as Drake, Migos and Travis Scott. DJ Pindz does’t just perform current urban anthems he also takes it back in time and performs old school urban classics such as Kanye West, Snoop-Dog and many more. Take a look at contact details if you want him to perform at your Nightclub.
